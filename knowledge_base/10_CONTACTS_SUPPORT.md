**Response Time:** Immediate to 24 hours

- Basic technical guidance
- Order status

## Emergency Contacts

### Safety Issues
If paint causes skin irritation or health issue:
1. Wash affected area with soap and water
2. If eye contact, rinse with water for 15 minutes
3. Seek medical attention if symptoms persist
4. Report to: safety@birlaopus.com

### Quality Emergencies
Suspected major product defect:
1. Stop using the product immediately
2. Keep sample with batch details
3. Call 1800-103-7171 immediately
4. Sample collection within 48 hours
