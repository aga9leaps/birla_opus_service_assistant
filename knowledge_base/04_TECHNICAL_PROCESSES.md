# Technical Processes - Painting Guide

## Surface Preparation

### New Interior Walls (Plastered)

**Requirements:**
- Plaster must be cured minimum 28 days
- Surface must be dry (no dampness)
- No loose particles or dust

**Process:**
1. **Cleaning**
   - Remove dust with dry brush
   - Wipe with slightly damp cloth
   - Allow to dry completely

2. **Putty Application (Birla Opus Wall Putty)**
   - Mix putty with water to smooth paste
   - Apply first coat with putty blade (1-2mm thick)
   - Allow 4-6 hours drying
   - Sand with 180 grit sandpaper
   - Apply second coat
   - Allow 4-6 hours drying
   - Final sand with 220 grit

3. **Primer (Birla Opus Interior Primer)**
   - Dilute: 1L primer + 400-500ml water
   - Apply one coat with brush/roller
   - Coverage: 80-100 sq ft/L
   - Drying time: 4-6 hours

4. **Paint Application**
   - Dilute: 1L paint + 400-500ml water (first coat)
   - Dilute: 1L paint + 300-400ml water (second coat)
   - Apply 2 coats minimum
   - Gap between coats: 4-6 hours

---

### Old Interior Walls (Repaint)

**Assess Surface First:**
- Is old paint in good condition? → Direct repaint
- Is paint peeling/flaking? → Scrape and repair
- Is there dampness? → Fix water source first

**Process for Good Surface:**
1. Clean walls with damp cloth
2. Sand lightly with 220 grit
3. Spot-apply putty on damaged areas
4. Apply 2 coats of paint

**Process for Damaged Surface:**
1. Scrape all loose paint
2. Sand entire surface (180 grit)
3. Apply putty on damaged areas
4. Apply primer
5. Apply 2 coats of paint

**Dark to Light Color Change:**
- Apply 1 coat white primer first
- Then 2-3 coats of final color

---

### Exterior Walls (New)

**Requirements:**
- Plaster cured minimum 28 days
- Check for hairline cracks
- Ensure proper waterproofing if needed

**Process:**
1. **Cleaning**
   - Pressure wash if possible
   - Remove algae, moss, loose particles
   - Allow 24-48 hours drying

2. **Crack Treatment**
   - Fill cracks with Birla Opus Crack Filler
   - Allow 24 hours curing
   - Sand smooth

3. **Primer (Birla Opus Exterior Primer)**
   - Apply one coat
   - Coverage: 70-90 sq ft/L
   - Drying time: 6-8 hours

4. **Paint Application**
   - Dilute: 1L + 300-400ml water
   - Apply 2-3 coats
   - Gap between coats: 6-8 hours
   - Final coat: minimal dilution for better durability

---

### Exterior Walls (Repaint)

**Process:**
1. Pressure wash entire surface
2. Scrape loose/flaking paint
3. Treat algae areas with Birla Opus Algae Wash
4. Fill cracks
5. Apply primer on bare patches
6. Apply 2 coats exterior paint

**If Extensive Peeling:**
- Full scraping required
- Apply primer on entire surface
- May need 3 coats of paint

## Application Techniques

### Brush Application
**Best for:** Edges, corners, small areas, touch-ups

**Technique:**
- Dip brush 1/3 into paint
- Remove excess on container edge
- Apply in long, even strokes
- Finish with light strokes in one direction

### Roller Application
**Best for:** Large flat areas, walls, ceilings

**Technique:**
- Pour paint into tray
- Load roller evenly
- Apply in 'W' pattern first
- Then fill in with vertical strokes
- Maintain wet edge to avoid lap marks

### Spray Application
**Best for:** Large exterior areas, smooth finish

**Note:** Requires specialized equipment and training. Not recommended for DIY.

## Dilution Guidelines

### Water-Based Products (All Birla Opus Emulsions)

| Coat | Dilution Ratio | Notes |
|------|----------------|-------|
| First Coat | 1L paint : 400-500ml water | More diluted for better penetration |
| Second Coat | 1L paint : 300-400ml water | Less diluted for better coverage |
| Final Coat | 1L paint : 200-300ml water | Minimal dilution for best finish |

**Important:**
- Use only clean water
- Mix thoroughly before application
- Do not over-dilute (causes poor coverage)
- Never use thinner or other solvents

## Weather Conditions

### Ideal Conditions
- Temperature: 15-35°C
- Humidity: 40-70%
- No rain for 4 hours after application

### Do NOT Paint When
- Temperature below 10°C
- Temperature above 45°C
- Humidity above 85%
- Rain expected within 4 hours
- Strong winds (for exterior)
- Direct harsh sunlight (causes uneven drying)

### Best Time for Exterior Painting
- Season: October to March (avoid monsoon)
- Time of day: 7-11 AM or 3-6 PM (avoid midday sun)

## Special Surfaces

### Ceiling
- Use ceiling-specific paint or interior emulsion
- Apply in single direction
- Use extension pole for roller
- Work in small sections

### Bathroom (Non-waterproofed)
- Ensure good ventilation
- Use anti-fungal paint
- Apply extra coat near water areas

### Kitchen
- Use washable paint (Calista Ever Wash recommended)
- Apply extra coat near cooking area
- Easy to clean grease and stains
