# Troubleshooting Guide - Paint Problems & Solutions

## Paint Failures

### 1. Peeling / Flaking

**Symptoms:**
- Paint coming off in sheets or flakes
- Usually starts at edges or near moisture areas

**Causes:**
1. Moisture/dampness in wall
2. Poor surface preparation
3. Painted on dusty/oily surface
4. Skipped primer on new walls
5. Incompatible paint layers

**Fix Procedure:**
1. Scrape all loose paint completely
2. Check for dampness with moisture meter
3. If damp: Fix water source, apply waterproofing
4. Sand edges smooth (180 grit)
5. Apply Birla Opus Exterior/Interior Primer
6. Wait 6-8 hours
7. Apply 2 coats of paint

**Prevention:**
- Always check moisture before painting
- Never skip primer on bare walls
- Ensure surface is clean and dry
- Use correct primer for surface type

---

### 2. Bubbling / Blistering

**Symptoms:**
- Raised bumps or bubbles on paint surface
- May burst when touched, revealing bare wall

**Causes:**
1. Moisture trapped under paint
2. Painting in hot sun (exterior)
3. Poor ventilation during drying
4. Oil/grease on surface before painting

**Fix Procedure:**
1. Scrape off all bubbled paint
2. Identify and fix moisture source
3. Allow wall to dry completely (2-3 days)
4. Sand smooth
5. Apply primer
6. Repaint with proper technique

**Prevention:**
- Never paint in direct sunlight
- Ensure wall is completely dry
- Clean surface thoroughly before painting
- Maintain proper ventilation

---

### 3. Cracking / Crazing

**Symptoms:**
- Fine cracks in paint surface (like dried mud)
- May be surface-level or deep

**Types:**
- **Hairline cracks:** Surface level, cosmetic
- **Alligator cracks:** Deep, looks like alligator skin
- **Structural cracks:** From wall movement

**Causes:**
1. Too thick paint application
2. Paint not fully dry between coats
3. Low quality or old paint
4. Building settlement/movement
5. Extreme temperature changes

**Fix Procedure:**
For surface cracks:
1. Sand affected area
2. Apply thin coat of paint

For deep cracks:
1. Scrape affected area
2. Fill with Birla Opus Crack Filler
3. Allow 24 hours curing
4. Prime and repaint

For structural cracks:
1. Fix structural issue first
2. Use flexible crack filler
3. Apply fiberglass tape over crack
4. Putty, prime, and paint

---

### 4. Chalking

**Symptoms:**
- Powdery residue on surface when touched
- Common on exterior walls after years

**Causes:**
1. Natural weathering of paint
2. Low quality paint
3. Over-thinning of paint
4. Applying paint too thin

**Fix Procedure:**
1. Wash surface with water to remove chalk
2. Allow to dry completely
3. Apply Birla Opus Exterior Primer
4. Apply 2 coats of exterior paint

**Prevention:**
- Use quality exterior paint (Calista Neo Star or above)
- Don't over-dilute paint
- Apply recommended number of coats

---

### 5. Fading / Discoloration

**Symptoms:**
- Color becoming lighter or changing tone
- Usually on exterior or sun-facing walls

**Causes:**
1. UV exposure (sunlight)
2. Low quality pigments
3. Alkaline surface attacking paint
4. Pollution and environmental factors

**Fix Procedure:**
1. Clean surface
2. Apply primer if needed
3. Repaint with UV-resistant paint (One True Vision recommended)

**Prevention:**
- Use high-quality exterior paint with UV protection
- One True Vision has 16-year fade resistance

---

### 6. Efflorescence (White Salt Deposits)

**Symptoms:**
- White crystalline deposits on wall surface
- Usually on exterior walls or near ground level

**Causes:**
1. Salts in building materials coming to surface
2. Moisture carrying salts through wall
3. Poor waterproofing

**Fix Procedure:**
1. Remove deposits with wire brush (dry)
2. Do NOT wash with water (spreads salts)
3. Identify and fix moisture source
4. Apply Birla Opus Alldry Salt Seal
5. Then apply exterior paint

**Prevention:**
- Use Alldry Salt Seal as primer in salt-prone areas
- Ensure proper waterproofing
- Fix any water seepage issues

---

### 7. Algae / Fungus Growth

**Symptoms:**
- Green, black, or brown patches on walls
- Common in humid/shaded areas

**Causes:**
1. High humidity
2. Poor ventilation
3. Water seepage
4. Shaded areas that stay damp

**Fix Procedure:**
1. Scrub affected area with Birla Opus Algae Wash
2. Allow to dry completely (24 hours)
3. Apply anti-fungal primer
4. Use anti-fungal paint (all Calista range has this)

**Prevention:**
- Ensure good drainage around building
- Improve ventilation
- Use anti-fungal paint in humid areas
- Fix any water leakage

---

### 8. Poor Coverage / Patchy Finish

**Symptoms:**
- Old color showing through
- Uneven color on wall

**Causes:**
1. Insufficient coats applied
2. Over-dilution of paint
3. Low quality paint
4. Dark to light color without primer

**Fix Procedure:**
1. Apply additional coat(s) of paint
2. Use less dilution
3. If dark to light, apply white primer first

**Prevention:**
- Follow recommended dilution ratios
- Apply minimum 2 coats
- Use primer when changing colors dramatically

---

### 9. Roller Marks / Brush Strokes

**Symptoms:**
- Visible lines or texture from application tool
- Uneven finish

**Causes:**
1. Paint drying too fast
2. Poor technique
3. Low quality tools
4. Not maintaining wet edge

**Fix Procedure:**
1. Light sand with 400 grit
2. Apply another coat with proper technique
3. Work in smaller sections
4. Maintain wet edge

**Prevention:**
- Don't paint in hot conditions
- Use good quality rollers/brushes
- Work in manageable sections
- Keep wet edge while painting

---

### 10. Sagging / Drips

**Symptoms:**
- Paint running down in drips or sags
- Wave-like pattern on vertical surfaces

**Causes:**
1. Too much paint applied at once
2. Over-dilution
3. Painting on glossy surface without sanding

**Fix Procedure:**
1. Let dry completely
2. Sand drips smooth
3. Apply thin, even coat

**Prevention:**
- Apply thin coats
- Don't overload brush/roller
- Sand glossy surfaces before painting
