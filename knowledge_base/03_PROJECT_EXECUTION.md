# Project Execution Guide

## Pre-Project Checklist

### Before Starting Any Job

**For Dealer:**
- [ ] Quote approved and signed by customer
- [ ] 30% advance payment received
- [ ] Start date confirmed with customer
- [ ] Painter assigned and confirmed
- [ ] Material ordered/available in stock

**For Painter:**
- [ ] Site visit done, understand scope
- [ ] Material list received from dealer
- [ ] Tools and equipment ready
- [ ] Customer contact number saved
- [ ] Access arrangements confirmed (keys, timings)

## Day-by-Day Workflow

### Interior Painting (Fresh - 1 Room)

**Day 1: Preparation**
- Morning: Shift furniture to center, cover with sheets
- Cover floor with plastic/newspaper
- Cover electrical points, switches
- Afternoon: Sand walls lightly (180 grit)
- Clean walls with damp cloth
- Fill minor cracks with putty
- Evening: Clean up, brief customer on progress

**Day 2: Putty Application**
- Morning: Apply first coat of wall putty
- Use Birla Opus Acrylic Wall Putty
- Apply with putty blade, keep 1-2mm thickness
- Afternoon: Allow 4-6 hours drying
- Check for any missed spots
- Evening: Update customer

**Day 3: Putty Sanding + Second Coat**
- Morning: Sand first putty coat (180 grit)
- Remove dust with dry cloth
- Apply second coat of putty
- Afternoon: Allow drying
- Evening: Check smoothness

**Day 4: Primer Application**
- Morning: Final sanding (220 grit)
- Clean surface thoroughly
- Apply primer coat (Birla Opus Interior Primer)
- Dilute: 1L primer + 500ml water
- Afternoon: Allow 4-6 hours drying
- Evening: Inspect for any defects

**Day 5: Paint - First Coat**
- Morning: Apply first coat of paint
- Dilute: 1L paint + 400-500ml water
- Use roller for large areas, brush for edges
- Afternoon: Allow 4-6 hours drying
- Evening: Check coverage, note touch-up areas

**Day 6: Paint - Final Coat + Handover**
- Morning: Light sanding if needed (400 grit)
- Apply final coat of paint
- Afternoon: Touch-ups, clean edges
- Remove masking tape carefully
- Evening: Clean up, remove covers
- Rearrange furniture
- Customer inspection and handover

---

### Exterior Painting Timeline

| Phase | Duration | Activities |
|-------|----------|------------|
| Preparation | 1-2 days | Scaffolding, pressure wash, crack repair |
| Primer | 1 day | Apply exterior primer, dry overnight |
| First Coat | 1-2 days | Apply first coat, cover all areas |
| Second Coat | 1-2 days | Apply second coat |
| Touch-up | 1 day | Edges, corners, cleanup |

**Weather Note:** Do not paint if:
- Rain expected within 4 hours
- Temperature below 10°C or above 45°C
- Humidity above 85%

## Quality Checkpoints

### After Putty
- [ ] Surface is smooth to touch
- [ ] No visible cracks or holes
- [ ] Edges and corners are clean
- [ ] No putty on woodwork or fittings

### After Primer
- [ ] Uniform coverage, no bare patches
- [ ] No drips or runs
- [ ] Proper drying (not tacky)
- [ ] Surface ready for paint

### After First Coat
- [ ] Even color distribution
- [ ] No roller marks or brush strokes
- [ ] Coverage is adequate
- [ ] No drips or sags

### Final Inspection (Before Handover)
- [ ] Color matches customer's selection
- [ ] Finish is uniform (sheen consistency)
- [ ] Edges are clean and sharp
- [ ] No paint on switches, fittings, floor
- [ ] All touch-ups completed
- [ ] Area is clean and tidy

### Issue Escalation
If any problem:
1. Inform customer immediately
2. Inform dealer
3. Propose solution
4. Get approval before proceeding

**Common Issues:**
- Dampness found behind wall → Need waterproofing (additional cost)
- Old paint peeling extensively → Need full scraping (more time)
- Color looks different on wall → Normal, will look right after 2nd coat
- Customer wants to change color → Can do, may need extra coat

## Material Management

### Material Requisition
Before starting job:
1. Calculate material needed (from quote)
2. Add 10% buffer for wastage
3. Collect from dealer or get delivered to site

### On-Site Storage
- Store paint cans in cool, dry place
- Keep away from direct sunlight
- Ensure lids are tight when not in use
- Keep away from children

### Leftover Material
- Seal properly, return to dealer
- Label with customer name and date
- Can be used for touch-ups later

---

## Safety Guidelines

### For Painters
- Use mask when sanding
- Use goggles for ceiling work
- Ensure ladder/scaffolding is stable
- Keep area ventilated
- Wash hands before eating
- Don't smoke on site

### For Customer Property
- Cover all furniture completely
- Protect floor with thick plastic
- Remove or cover AC units
- Cover electrical appliances
- Be careful around glass/mirrors

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Impact | Prevention |
|---------|--------|------------|
| Skipping primer | Paint peels within months | Always apply primer on bare walls |
| Painting on damp wall | Bubbling, peeling | Check moisture before starting |
| Not enough drying time | Poor finish, tacky surface | Wait minimum hours between coats |
| Over-diluting paint | Poor coverage, runs | Follow recommended dilution |
| Painting in direct sun | Uneven drying, lap marks | Paint in shade or early/late hours |
| Not covering properly | Paint on furniture/floor | Spend time on proper covering |
