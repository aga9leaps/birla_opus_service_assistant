# Birla Opus PaintCraft - Service Overview

## What is PaintCraft?

PaintCraft is Birla Opus's dealer-led professional painting service program. It enables authorized dealers to offer complete painting solutions to customers - not just selling paint, but providing end-to-end painting services.

**Tagline:** "Painting Made Easy"

---

## How PaintCraft Works

### The Service Model

```
Customer Need → Dealer (Service Partner) → Certified Painters → Quality Delivery
```

1. **Customer contacts dealer** for painting requirement
2. **Dealer provides** consultation, site survey, quotation
3. **Dealer assigns** certified Birla Opus ID painters
4. **Painters execute** the project with Birla Opus products
5. **Dealer ensures** quality, handles payments, provides warranty

---

## Key Stakeholders

### 1. Dealer (PaintCraft Partner)
- Owns the customer relationship
- Provides quotation and project management
- Earns margin on paint + service charges
- Responsible for quality and warranty

### 2. Painter/Contractor (Birla Opus ID Member)
- Certified and trained by Birla Opus
- Gets jobs through dealer network
- Earns labor charges
- Follows Birla Opus quality standards

### 3. Sales Team
- Onboards dealers to PaintCraft program
- Supports dealers in growing services business
- Provides training and marketing support

## PaintCraft Service Process

### Step 1: Customer Inquiry
- Customer visits dealer shop
- Customer calls dealer
- Referral from Birla Opus (1800-103-7171)

### Step 2: Site Survey (Free)
- Dealer or painter visits site
- Measures paintable area
- Assesses surface condition
- Identifies special requirements (waterproofing, repairs)

### Step 3: Quotation
- Itemized quote with:
  - Material cost (Birla Opus products)
  - Labor cost
  - Consumables
  - Service charges
- Valid for 15 days

### Step 4: Booking
- Customer approves quote
- Pays 30% advance
- Confirms start date

### Step 5: Execution
- Painter mobilization
- Surface preparation
- Paint application
- Quality checkpoints
- Daily progress updates

### Step 6: Handover
- Final inspection with customer
- Touch-ups if needed
- Collect balance payment
- Provide warranty card

### Step 7: Post-Service
- Feedback call within 3 days
- Warranty support as needed

## Service Warranty

### Workmanship Warranty (1 Year)
Covers:
- Peeling or flaking due to application defect
- Uneven finish or color inconsistency
- Touch-up for minor issues

Does NOT Cover:
- Structural defects (building cracks, settlement)
- Water damage from leakage (if waterproofing not done)
- Customer-caused damage
- Normal wear and tear

### Product Warranty (As per product)
- Interior paints: Up to 7 years
- Exterior paints: Up to 16 years
- Waterproofing: Up to 10 years
