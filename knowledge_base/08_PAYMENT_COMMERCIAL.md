# Payment & Commercial Terms

## Service Payment Structure

### Standard Payment Terms (Customer)

| Milestone | Payment | When |
|-----------|---------|------|
| Booking | 30% Advance | On quote acceptance |
| Mid-project | 40% | After surface prep complete |
| Completion | 30% Balance | After final inspection |

**Alternative (Small Jobs under Rs. 25,000):**
- 50% Advance on booking
- 50% on completion

---

## Customer Payment Options

### Accepted Payment Methods
1. **Cash** - Provide receipt
2. **UPI** - GPay, PhonePe, Paytm to dealer account
3. **Bank Transfer** - NEFT/RTGS/IMPS
4. **Cheque** - Allow 3 working days for clearance before starting
5. **Credit/Debit Card** - If dealer has POS machine

### EMI Options (Large Projects)
For projects above Rs. 50,000:
- Partner with Bajaj Finserv, HDFC, ICICI
- 3-6-12 month EMI options
- Process through dealer

## Dealer Earnings

### Revenue Components

```
Total Project Revenue
├── Material Margin (10-18% on Birla Opus products)
├── Labor Markup (15-25% on painter rates)
└── Service Fee (10-15% on total project)
```

### Example Calculation

**Project: 2BHK Interior (1,200 sq ft) - Rs. 45,000 total**

| Component | Amount | Dealer Earning |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| Material Cost | Rs. 15,000 | Rs. 2,250 (15% margin) |
| Labor Cost (to painter) | Rs. 18,000 | - |
| Labor Charged (to customer) | Rs. 22,000 | Rs. 4,000 (markup) |
| Service Fee | Rs. 8,000 | Rs. 8,000 |
| **Total Dealer Earning** | - | **Rs. 14,250** |

**Dealer Earning %:** ~32% of project value

## Painter Payment Terms

### Payment to Painters

| Payment Model | Details |
|---------------|---------|
| **Per Sq Ft** | Most common, rate varies by work type |
| **Daily Rate** | Rs. 600-1000/day for skilled painter |

| Work Type | Rate to Painter |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Interior Fresh (putty+primer+paint) | Rs. 14-18/sq ft |
| Interior Repaint | Rs. 8-12/sq ft |
| Exterior Fresh | Rs. 16-22/sq ft |
| Exterior Repaint | Rs. 10-16/sq ft |
| Waterproofing | Rs. 25-35/sq ft |

### Payment Schedule to Painters

| Milestone | Payment |
|-----------|---------|
| After surface prep | 30% |
| After first coat | 30% |
| After completion + customer approval | 40% |

**Note:** Only pay final 40% after customer signs off on completion.

## Birla Opus ID - Painter Loyalty Program

### What is Birla Opus ID?
Exclusive program for registered painters to earn cashback on Birla Opus purchases.

### How It Works
1. Painter registers on Birla Opus ID app
2. When buying Birla Opus products, scan QR coupon
3. Cashback credited to wallet/bank account
4. Monthly payout

### Cashback Rates

| Painter Level | Annual Purchase | Cashback Rate |
|---------------|-----------------|---------------|
| Bronze | Up to Rs. 2 Lakhs | 5% |
| Silver | Rs. 2-5 Lakhs | 7% |
| Gold | Rs. 5-10 Lakhs | 10% |
| Platinum | Rs. 10+ Lakhs | 12% |

### Additional Benefits (Gold & Above)
- Health insurance coverage
- Skill training programs
- Priority job leads
- Annual rewards trip (Platinum)

## Credit Terms

### Dealer Credit from Birla Opus
- Credit period: 15-30 days (based on assessment)
- Credit limit: Based on purchase history and security
- Early payment discount: 0.5% for payment within 5 days

### Customer Credit (Not Recommended)
- Avoid giving credit to customers
- If necessary, limit to known/repeat customers
- Maximum 7 days post-completion
- Get post-dated cheque

---

## Invoicing

### To Customer
Invoice should include:
1. Customer name and address
2. Site address
3. Detailed scope of work
4. Material list with quantities
5. Labor charges
6. GST (18%)
7. Payment terms
8. Warranty terms

### GST Compliance
- Painting services: 18% GST
- Paint products: 28% GST (included in MRP)
- For service billing, dealer must have GST registration

---

## Refund Policy

### When Refund Applies
1. Job cancelled before starting - Full advance refund minus 5% processing
2. Job cancelled mid-way - Refund for incomplete portion minus material used
3. Quality issue not resolved - Partial refund based on assessment

### Refund Process
1. Customer raises request
2. Dealer assesses situation
3. Calculate refund amount
4. Process within 7 working days
5. Document reason for records

---

## Dispute Resolution

### Payment Disputes with Customers
1. Review original quote and agreement
2. Document actual work done
3. Try to negotiate amicably
4. Offer reasonable settlement
5. Escalate to Birla Opus if unresolved

### Common Dispute Scenarios

| Scenario | Resolution |
|----------|------------|
| Customer refuses final payment claiming quality issue | Inspect, fix issues, then collect payment |
| Customer says quote was lower | Show signed quote, explain any additions |
| Customer wants discount after completion | Generally don't, offer future discount instead |

## Record Keeping

### Essential Records
1. **Quote** - Signed by customer
2. **Advance Receipt** - With payment mode
3. **Material Indent** - What was ordered
4. **Completion Sign-off** - Customer signature
5. **Final Invoice** - With payment received
6. **Warranty Card** - Copy with dealer

### Retention Period
- Keep all records for minimum 3 years
- Digital backup recommended

## Financial Tips for Dealers

### Pricing Strategy
- Don't compete only on price
- Highlight value: quality, warranty, Birla Opus brand
- Be transparent in quotes
- Include buffer for unexpected issues

### Cash Flow Management
- Collect advance before buying material
- Don't start work without advance
- Track painter payments carefully
- Invoice promptly on completion

### Profit Maximization
- Push premium products (higher margins)
- Offer complete solutions (paint + waterproofing)
- Build repeat customer base
- Get referrals from satisfied customers
