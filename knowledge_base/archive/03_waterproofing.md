## Application Process

### External Wall Waterproofing
1. **Surface Preparation**
   - Remove loose paint, dust, algae
   - Clean with water jet
   - Allow surface to dry (24-48 hours)

2. **Crack Treatment**
   - Fill cracks with ALLDRY Repair Master
   - Allow 24 hours curing

3. **Primer Application** (if needed)
   - Apply Opus Exterior Primer on porous surfaces
   - Allow 6-8 hours drying

4. **Waterproofing Application**
   - Apply first coat uniformly
   - Allow 6-8 hours drying
   - Apply second coat perpendicular to first

5. **Topcoat** (optional)
   - Apply exterior emulsion after 24 hours

### Terrace Waterproofing
1. Clean terrace of debris, dust, loose material
2. Fill cracks and joints
3. Apply primer if surface is porous
4. Apply waterproofing in 2-3 coats
5. Create proper slope toward drain
6. Cure for 7 days before exposure

## Troubleshooting Guide

### Problem: Water seepage through walls
**Likely Cause:** Cracks, porous surface, no waterproofing
**Solution:** Apply ALLDRY Wall n Roof after crack repair

### Problem: Terrace leakage
**Likely Cause:** Cracks in slab, damaged waterproofing, poor drainage
**Solution:** Apply ALLDRY Total 2K, ensure proper drainage

### Problem: Rising damp (water marks at bottom of wall)
**Likely Cause:** Ground water rising through foundation
**Solution:** Apply ALLDRY Dampstop with injection method

### Problem: Bathroom tile grout seepage
**Likely Cause:** Failed grout, no membrane under tiles
**Solution:** For new work - apply ALLDRY Bathroom under tiles
