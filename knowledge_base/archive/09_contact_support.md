## Emergency Contacts

### Product Safety
**If paint causes skin irritation or allergic reaction:**
1. Wash affected area with soap and water
2. If eye contact, rinse with water for 15 minutes
3. If symptoms persist, consult doctor
4. Report to: safety@birlaopus.com

### Quality Complaints
**Suspected product defect:**
1. Stop using product
2. Keep sample for testing
3. Call 1800-120-1234 immediately
4. Our team will collect sample within 48 hours
