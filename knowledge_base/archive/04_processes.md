# Birla Opus - Processes & Procedures

## For Dealers

### Dealer Onboarding Process

#### Step 1: Application
- Contact Birla Opus regional office or call 1800-120-1234
- Submit application with:
  - Business registration documents (GST, PAN, Shop Act)
  - Address proof of shop
  - Bank account details
  - Photographs of shop (exterior and interior)
  - 2 reference letters from existing paint dealers

#### Step 2: Verification
- Birla Opus team visits shop location
- Evaluates shop size (minimum 200 sq ft display area required)
- Checks location accessibility and parking
- Assesses market potential

#### Step 3: Agreement
- Sign dealership agreement (1-year renewable)
- Submit security deposit (varies by region)
- Receive dealer code and login credentials

#### Step 4: Setup
- Receive Birla Opus signage and branding materials
- Install color display racks (provided on loan)
- Training on product range (2-day program)
- Access to dealer portal and mobile app

#### Step 5: First Order
- Minimum opening stock: Rs. 2-5 lakhs (depending on market)
- Delivery within 4 hours in depot towns
- Credit period: 15-30 days (based on assessment)

### Painting Process - Standard

#### Interior Painting (New Surface)
1. **Surface Inspection**
   - Check for dampness, cracks, unevenness
   - Ensure plastering is fully cured (minimum 28 days)

2. **Surface Preparation**
   - Remove loose particles with sandpaper (180 grit)
   - Clean with damp cloth, allow to dry

3. **Putty Application** (2 coats)
   - Apply Opus Acrylic Wall Putty with putty blade
   - First coat: Allow 4-6 hours drying
   - Sand with 180 grit paper
   - Second coat: Allow 4-6 hours drying
   - Final sanding with 220 grit

4. **Primer Application** (1 coat)
   - Apply Opus Interior Primer with brush/roller
   - Coverage: 80-100 sq ft/litre
   - Drying time: 4-6 hours

5. **Paint Application** (2 coats)
   - First coat: Apply with brush/roller
   - Drying time: 4-6 hours
   - Light sanding (optional) with 400 grit
   - Final coat: Apply uniformly

**Total Time:** 3-4 days for a room

---

#### Exterior Painting (New Surface)
1. **Surface Inspection**
   - Check for dampness, algae, efflorescence
   - Ensure waterproofing is done if needed

2. **Surface Preparation**
   - Pressure wash to remove dust and algae
   - Allow 48 hours drying
   - Fill cracks with Opus Crack Fill

3. **Primer Application**
   - Apply Opus Exterior Primer
   - Coverage: 70-90 sq ft/litre
   - Drying: 6-8 hours

4. **Paint Application** (2-3 coats)
   - First coat with brush/roller
   - Drying: 6-8 hours
   - Subsequent coats

**Best Time:** October to March (avoid monsoon)

---

### Repainting Process

#### Interior Repainting
1. Clean walls with damp cloth
2. Sand glossy surfaces lightly
3. Spot-apply putty on damaged areas
4. Apply 2 coats of emulsion

**Note:** Primer needed only if:
- Changing from dark to light color
- Old paint is peeling
- Surface is chalky

#### Exterior Repainting
1. Pressure wash surface
2. Scrape loose/flaking paint
3. Treat algae with Opus Algae Wash
4. Apply primer on bare patches
5. Apply 2 coats exterior emulsion

## For Sales Team

### Lead Management Process

1. **Lead Capture**
   - Website inquiry forms
   - Toll-free calls (1800-120-1234)
   - Walk-ins at Paint Studios
   - Dealer referrals
   - Contractor referrals

2. **Lead Assignment**
   - Auto-assignment based on location
   - Within 2 hours of lead capture
   - Notification to sales representative

3. **First Contact**
   - Call customer within 4 hours
   - Understand requirement (product/service)
   - Schedule site visit if needed

4. **Site Visit** (for projects)
   - Assess surface condition
   - Measure area
   - Recommend products
   - Provide quotation

5. **Follow-up**
   - Send quotation within 24 hours
   - Follow up every 3 days
   - Close or mark lost with reason

6. **Conversion**
   - Collect advance (30% for services)
   - Schedule execution
   - Handover to operations

## PaintCraft Services Process

### Service Request
1. Customer calls 1800-120-1234 or books online
2. Lead assigned to local PaintCraft team
3. Free site survey scheduled (within 48 hours)

### Site Survey
1. Surface inspection
2. Area measurement
3. Color consultation
4. Material estimation
5. Quote generation (same day)

### Quotation
Includes:
- Detailed scope of work
- Material list with quantities
- Labor charges
- Timeline
- Warranty terms

### Execution
1. Customer approves quote, pays 30% advance
2. Material delivered to site
3. Surface preparation (Day 1-2)
4. Painting execution (Day 3-5)
5. Touch-ups and cleanup (Day 6)
6. Customer inspection and sign-off

### Post-Service
- Feedback call within 3 days
- 1-year service warranty
- Touch-up service if needed
