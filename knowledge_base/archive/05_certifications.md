### Product Warranty

| Product Category | Warranty Period | Coverage |
|-----------------|-----------------|----------|
| Ultra Premium Interior | 7 years | Peeling, flaking, color fade |
| Premium Interior | 5 years | Peeling, flaking |
| Standard Interior | 3 years | Peeling, flaking |
| Ultra Premium Exterior | 10 years | Peeling, flaking, color fade |
| Premium Exterior | 7 years | Peeling, flaking, color fade |
| Standard Exterior | 5 years | Peeling, flaking |
| Waterproofing | 10-14 years | Water seepage |

### Warranty Conditions
- Must be applied as per recommended process
- Surface preparation must be proper
- Minimum recommended coats applied
- Purchase bill/invoice required for claims

### Warranty Exclusions
- Structural defects (cracks from building movement)
- Damage from natural disasters
- Improper application
- Use of unauthorized thinners
- Normal wear and tear

### Warranty Claim Process
1. Call 1800-120-1234 or email opuscare@adityabirla.com
2. Provide purchase details and photos of issue
3. Technical team visit within 7 days
4. Assessment report generated
5. If valid claim: repair/repaint at company cost
6. Resolution within 30 days
