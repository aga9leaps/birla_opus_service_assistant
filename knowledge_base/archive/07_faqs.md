## Application Questions

### Q: How many coats are needed?
**A:**
- New walls: 1 primer + 2 paint coats
- Repaint (good surface): 2 paint coats
- Dark to light color: 1 primer + 2-3 paint coats

### Q: What is the drying time?
**A:**
- Touch dry: 30 minutes to 2 hours
- Recoat: 4-8 hours (interior) / 6-12 hours (exterior)
- Full cure: 7-14 days

### Q: Can I use any thinner with Birla Opus paints?
**A:** Birla Opus water-based paints should only be thinned with clean water (10-15% maximum). Using other thinners will void warranty.

### Q: What weather is suitable for exterior painting?
**A:**
- Temperature: 10°C to 40°C
- Humidity: Below 85%
- Avoid: Rain, heavy dew, strong direct sunlight

### Q: How do I calculate paint requirement?
**A:**
1. Measure wall area (Length × Height) in sq ft
2. Subtract doors/windows area
3. Divide by coverage rate
4. Multiply by number of coats

Example: 500 sq ft wall ÷ 120 sq ft/L × 2 coats = 8.3L
Order 10L to be safe.
