## Application Guidelines

### Surface Preparation (Exterior)
1. Remove loose paint, dust, algae growth
2. Treat any dampness or seepage issues first
3. Repair cracks with Opus Crack Fill
4. Apply waterproofing if needed (see waterproofing section)
5. Apply exterior primer
6. Wait 6-8 hours before topcoat

### Weather Conditions for Application
- Temperature: 10°C to 40°C
- Humidity: Below 85%
- Avoid application during rain or if rain expected within 4 hours
- Avoid direct strong sunlight (best time: early morning or evening)

### Recommended Coats
- New construction: 1 primer + 2-3 exterior coats
- Repaint: Depends on surface condition

### Drying Times
- Touch dry: 1-2 hours
- Recoat: 6-8 hours
- Full cure: 14 days
