## Application Guidelines

### Surface Preparation (Interior)
1. Clean surface of dust, loose particles, grease
2. Fill cracks and holes with Opus Wall Putty
3. Sand smooth after putty dries (4-6 hours)
4. Apply primer coat, allow 4-6 hours drying
5. Apply first coat of emulsion
6. Light sanding between coats (optional)
7. Apply final coat

### Recommended Coats
- New walls: 1 primer + 2 emulsion coats
- Repaint (good condition): 2 emulsion coats
- Repaint (poor condition): 1 primer + 2 emulsion coats

### Drying Times
- Touch dry: 30 minutes to 1 hour
- Recoat: 4-6 hours
- Full cure: 7 days
